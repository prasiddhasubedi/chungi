About the Game

Chungee is a traditional foot-juggling game played by students in the parking area of Thapathali Engineering Campus, Kathmandu. Players sit in a circle and keep a small rubber ball airborne using only their feet.

🏢

Authentic Campus

Realistic 3D recreation of the Thapathali parking area, complete with Library, D Block, Material Lab and Tennis Court.

⚽

Realistic Physics

Accurate rubber ball simulation with gravity, bounce, friction, air drag and angular momentum.

👥

8 Characters

Choose from Prasiddha, Lakshya, Mandip, Resham, Hitesh, Prabesh, Manish or JD – each with unique stats.

🎮

6 Unique Moves

Basic Tap, Side Flick, Back Heel, Jump Pass, Spin Trick and Power Kick. Each with different risk and reward.

🌍

4 Game Modes

Classic, Rain (slippery), Night, and Hardcore mode for different challenges and atmospheres.

🏆

Combo System

Chain kicks without touching the ground for combo multipliers. Beat your friends' high scores!

How to Play
1️⃣

Select Character

Pick one of 8 characters. Each has different Power, Agility, and Spin ratings.

2️⃣

Choose Mode

Start with Classic mode. Rain, Night and Hardcore unlock new challenges.

3️⃣

Keep it Airborne

Press keys 1-6 or click move buttons to kick. Don't let Chungee touch the ground!

4️⃣

Build Combos

Kick multiple times without ground contact for multiplied score. Go for max combo!
